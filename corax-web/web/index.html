About

Gaurav and Nikhil Consultants Private Ltd, A Certified Public Accountants firm. It is a startup firm providing financial and taxation consultancy for Indian and US based companies. The firm is run by Mr. Gaurav Aggarwal (B.Com, FCA, CPA) and Mr. Nikhil Dhupar (B.com, FCA, CPA) The firm is on the panel of Punjab and Sind Bank, Union Bank of India, Central Bank of India, for its statutory, concurrent audits and other professional advice respectively. We mainly deal in Income Tax Matters, Statutory and Internal Audits, Finance, Service Tax, SEZ, etds Returns and other allied services.

Our Promoters

Ms Kanika Dhupar is managing director of the company. She is a Post graduate in Commerce and has a vast experience of dealing in Accounting and Financial solutions. She is handling the corporate affairs of the company.

Ms. Megha Aggarwal is a Post Graduate in Applied Sciences and is expert in managing a large scale industrial unit. She has 10 years of experience in the Education Industry.

Mr. Gaurav Aggarwal is an Indian Chartered Accountant by Profession. Mr. Nikhil Dhupar is an Indian Chartered Accountant by profession. He also qualified CPA (USA) and is an expert in Internal Audit, Project Financing and MIS Reporting and handling the teamwork. He has also worked in Banking Industry for 2.5 years and since 2009 he is doing certification work, accountancy work and internal audits.
